My hobbies

  • Music


    In short, almost my whole life I've dealt with music. I went to music school, almost finished it, but never did. Because of music, I've been to most of Europe, and South America. Currently, I'm a freelance musician, doing all sorts of gigs, from weddings, christenings, parties, birthdays, and I can tell you, it's not a bad job. It combines a lot of things I love, and I get to collaborate with people I want to work with.

  • Cooking


    I started living with roommates when I was 18 years old, and the first thing to learn correctly was cooking, 'cause, you gots to live, right. In the meantime, I fell in love with it, especially with any food that takes time and effort to prepare. So 5 hour stews, 4 hour under the lid (peka) beef, lamb with potatoes, military beans, barbecuing etc. We love to ccok outdoors in Croatia, especially with a beer in hand. Chilling with my friends, cooking is one of the best things. And the wife doesn't have to think about cooking, so that a marriage plus.

  • Cars, auto industry and moto races

    I'm sort of a petrol head, love driving, watching races, and tinkering on friends' cars. Since I have a Toyota, I don't have to tinker, because the damn thing doesn't even have the audacity to give me problems. I love attending drag races, moto shows, WRC Rally events and other. Since I had a lot of bad luck with cars, my last car being the exception, I have a lot of experience with repairing, diagnosing and giving my opinion to friends regarding cars, their budget and needs. So yeah.

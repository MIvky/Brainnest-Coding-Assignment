My favourite movies

Movies are fun, scary, sad and all gives us the whole emotional range. They are also a window to a cultures' soul. For instance, after watching Michael Bay movies, I know that citizens of USA love to blow shit up. And if you watch Croatian movies, don't. :) Kidding.
Movies are experienced with all senses, and open us to a possible new perspective. We all know the strengh of movies in modern times.

The list

  • The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

    • How can it not be the best movie trilogy of all times. The stunning visuals, fantastic story created in the mind of the gradpapa od fantasy genre. Jackson kept true to the source material (I'm looking at you, BS romace story between Tauriel and Kili.) This masterpiece of modern storytelling mixed with myths, legends still holds up, and I hope it will for a long, long time.

  • The Godfather

    • The film is based on Mario Puzo's The Godfather, which remained on The New York Times Best Seller list for 67 weeks and sold over nine million copies in two years. And after watching it once, you get hooked. You believe the story, despite the fact that the Puzzo of Godfather never even had contact with the M-word guys, you know, the construcion (good)fellas. He didn't know how their world looked. But he wrote a story so powerfull, that even the "construction guys" wanted in, just so they could control their public image. A perfect example of a high family drama, ambition, power and transformation.

  • Friday

    • Sometimes, you just want to chill and get away from all the sirious things around. That is when I turn on Friday. Ice Cube and Chris Tucker for the win. Buddy comedy filmed in 20 days, depicting problems in the hood. If you haven't seen it yet, check it out.
